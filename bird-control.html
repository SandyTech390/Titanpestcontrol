Humane Bird Proofing for Delhi NCR

Pigeons roost on balconies, AC ledges, solar panels and warehouses—droppings corrode surfaces, block drains and spread disease. Humane barriers like nets and spikes protect your property without harming birds.

Our Solutions

  • Balcony Netting: UV-stabilised, low-visibility, blocks openings.
  • Stainless-Steel Spikes: Stops perching on ledges & signage.
  • Solar Guards: Prevent nesting under solar arrays.
  • Discreet Deterrents: Visual/sound devices for hotspots.
  • Cleaning & Sanitisation: Guano removal and disinfection.

How We Work

  1. Free site survey & assessment.
  2. Itemised plan & transparent quote.
  3. Dropping removal & disinfection.
  4. Installation of nets, spikes or guards.
  5. Quality check & documentation.
  6. Handover + maintenance guidance.

Pricing Guide

Balcony netting averages ₹85/sq ft, spikes start from ₹2,500, solar proofing by array. Quotes depend on area, height & complexity. Free survey + itemised proposal provided.
