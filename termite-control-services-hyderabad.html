Permanent Termite Protection for New Constructions

Termites silently damage wooden structures, furniture, and foundations. To prevent costly infestations, TITAN Pest Control offers the Anti-Termite Reticulation System – a scientifically designed piping system installed at the pre-construction or renovation stage. It allows uniform chemical distribution beneath the floor and around the foundation, forming a long-lasting barrier against termites.

Why Choose Our Reticulation System?

  • 10-Year Warranty: Long-term protection with documented assurance.
  • Safe & Certified Chemicals: Govt.-approved, eco-safe termiticides.
  • Easy Maintenance: Refilling points allow quick recharging without drilling.
  • Permanent Protection: Prevents termites from reaching walls and flooring.
  • Ideal for Builders & Owners: Perfect for new buildings, housing societies, offices & warehouses.

Installation Process

  1. Planning & Layout: Mapping reticulation pipes across the foundation and flooring.
  2. Pipe Installation: Laying durable perforated pipes under flooring & around foundation walls.
  3. Chemical Filling: Introducing safe termiticide through the pipe network.
  4. Testing & Sealing: Ensuring uniform distribution and sealing points for future refills.
  5. Documentation: Service reports, warranty cards, and maintenance schedule provided.
