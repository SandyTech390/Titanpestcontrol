Trusted Termite Protection Services for New Buildings in Hyderabad

Termites can silently destroy wooden structures, furniture, and even building foundations, leading to costly repairs. To protect your property, Titan Pest Control provides the Anti-Termite Reticulation System – a scientifically designed piping system installed during the pre-construction or renovation stage. This system ensures uniform distribution of termite-safe chemicals beneath the flooring and around the foundation, creating a long-lasting protective barrier that prevents termite infestations and safeguards your investment.

Why Choose Titan Pest Control for Termite Control?

Titan Pest Control offers reliable and long-lasting termite control solutions designed to protect your home and property. Our trained and certified technicians use advanced techniques and government-approved chemicals for effective results. We focus on eliminating termites at the source while preventing future infestations. With inspection-led treatment plans, we ensure maximum coverage and safety. Our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction makes us a trusted choice for termite protection.

Installation Process

  1. Planning & Layout: Mapping reticulation pipes across the foundation and flooring.
  2. Pipe Installation: Laying durable perforated pipes under flooring & around foundation walls.
  3. Chemical Filling: Introducing safe termiticide through the pipe network.
  4. Testing & Sealing: Ensuring uniform distribution and sealing points for future refills.
  5. Documentation: Service reports, warranty cards, and maintenance schedule provided.
