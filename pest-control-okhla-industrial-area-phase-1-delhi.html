Industrial Services We Provide

  1. Commercial Cockroach Control: Gel baiting + residual sprays — canteens, utilities, drain lines & storage.

  4. Office Bed Bug Control: Discreet multi-visit protocol for seating/rest areas.
  5. Fly Management: UV ILTs, residuals & breeding-site elimination for food/organic handling units.
  6. Commercial Bird Control: Netting, spikes & exclusion for sheds and façades.
  7. Industrial Disinfection: High-touch zones, cabins & common areas hygiene programs.
  8. Stored Product Pest (SPP): Beetles/moths control in raw materials and finished goods.

Our Process (Built for Industry)
