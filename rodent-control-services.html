Expert Rat & Rodent Control with Safe & Guaranteed Solutions

Rodents such as rats and mice pose serious health risks and can cause extensive property damage. They chew electrical wires, contaminate food supplies, and spread dangerous diseases like leptospirosis and hantavirus. Due to their intelligence, cautious behavior, and ability to adapt quickly, DIY rodent control methods often provide only temporary relief. Titan Pest Control offers scientific and professional rodent control services in Hyderabad, designed to ensure rapid elimination and long-term prevention. Our expert technicians use proven techniques to target rodent activity at the source, helping protect your home or business from recurring infestations.

  • Certified Experts: Trained technicians with years of experience in rodent management.

Our Professional Rodent Control Methods

  1. Inspection: Identify nesting areas, burrows, food sources, and entry points.
  2. Sticky Pads: Safe glue boards placed in strategic spots inside rodent boxes.
  3. Baiting: Rodenticide baits placed in tamper-proof stations, effective for large infestations.
  4. Burrow Fumigation: Special cakes placed inside rodent burrows for quick elimination.
  5. Proofing Advice: Seal entry holes, improve sanitation, and secure food sources.

Signs You Need Rodent Control

  • Scratching noises at night inside walls or ceilings.
  • Gnaw marks on wires, furniture, or food packets.
  • Droppings in kitchens, storerooms, or corners.
  • Grease marks or tracks along walls and floors.
  • Unpleasant ammonia-like smell in closed areas.
