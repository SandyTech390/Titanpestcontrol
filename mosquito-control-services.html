Mosquito Control Services — Hyderabad

  • Home
  • Mosquito Control Services
Featured 4.8

Beat monsoon spikes & year-round mosquito nuisance with Titan. We cut adult populations fast, kill larvae at source, and build a protective barrier—safe, compliant, and result-driven across Hyderabad.

Treatment Methods

  • Fogging (ULV/Thermal): Rapid knockdown of active adults in outdoor/common areas.
  • Residual Spraying: Resting surfaces (walls, vegetation, shaded spots) for ongoing protection.
  • Larviciding: Treat non-drainable water & hotspots; source reduction guidance.

Dengue Prevention Focus

  • Identify & eliminate breeding sites (coolers, trays, pots, drains).
  • Eco-responsible formulations; targeted, low-exposure application.
  • Preventive steps: cover tanks, clean drains, use screens/repellents.

Process

  1. Inspection: Mapping breeding & resting zones.
  2. Treatment: Larviciding + fogging/spray as per site.
  3. Barrier Setup: Residual coverage for longevity.
  4. Follow-up: Monitoring & seasonal scheduling (AMC).

Pricing

  • Residential: from ₹999 (area & method based)
  • Societies/Commercial: inspection-based quote (per sqft/zone)
