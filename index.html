Common pests in Hyderabad include: Cockroaches (American: reddish-brown, 3-4cm; German: light brown, 1.5cm; look for droppings, musty odor). Termites (subterranean build mud tubes; look for wood damage, discarded wings post-monsoon). Mosquitoes (Culex, Anopheles near water; Aedes with white stripes carry dengue). Rodents (mice, rats; look for droppings, gnaw marks). Bed Bugs (rust-colored spots on bedding, itchy bites). Pest activity varies seasonally: cockroaches/mosquitoes surge in monsoon, rodents increase in winter.

Prepare for cockroach treatment in Hyderabad apartments: Clear and clean kitchen cabinets/countertops. Empty bathroom cabinets. Declutter floors and move furniture from walls (15-20cm). Secure food in airtight containers. Plan for temporary evacuation (3-4 hrs for gel, 6-8 hrs for spray). Protect sensitive items (fish tanks, pet food, toothbrushes). Fix leaky pipes. After treatment, avoid mopping near treated areas for 7-10 days and don't wash treated cabinets for 2 weeks. Wash food surfaces before use.

Optimal seasons for termite treatment in Hyderabad are Pre-monsoon (April-June) for preventative treatment as dry soil allows deeper penetration, and Winter (November-February) for comprehensive treatment as termites move deeper indoors. Avoid peak monsoon (July-August) due to rain washing away treatment and extreme summer (May-June >45°C) which can affect chemical efficacy. We recommend a two-phase approach: pre-monsoon barrier and winter structural treatment for best results in Hyderabad's climate.
