For homes and businesses in Hyderabad, we recommend a strategic pest control schedule to ensure year-round protection:

  • Quarterly General Pest Control: Conduct treatments every 3 months for consistent, year-round maintenance.
  • Annual Termite Inspection and Treatment: Schedule yearly inspections, with extra checks before and after the monsoon season.
  • Monthly Mosquito Control: Focus on peak mosquito season (July to October) for effective prevention.
  • Bi-Monthly Rodent Checks: Recommended during winter months (November to February) to control rodent activity.
  • Pre-Monsoon Treatments: Essential during May–June, as Hyderabad’s climate promotes pest infestations.
  • Homes Near Green Areas: May require more frequent treatments due to higher pest activity.

The most effective termite treatment in Hyderabad depends on the type of property and the stage of infestation. At Titan Pest Control, we recommend proven termite control methods tailored to local conditions:

  • Chemical Soil Treatment: Creates a protective barrier and is ideal for new constructions, especially effective in Hyderabad’s clay-rich soil.
  • Wood Treatment:Involves direct application to infested wood and is essential for older buildings and furniture protection.
  • Termite Baiting Systems:An eco-friendly solution that targets and eliminates the entire termite colony over time.
  • Microwave or Heat Treatment:A non-chemical option suitable for localized termite infestations in specific areas.
For long-lasting protection, Titan Pest Control often follows a combination approach, including soil treatment, wood treatment, and continuous monitoring. This comprehensive strategy provides effective defense against Hyderabad’s aggressive subterranean termites.
