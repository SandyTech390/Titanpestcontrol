Stop Bed Bugs Fast — Safe, Discreet & Guaranteed

Bed bugs thrive in dense city living and spread via travel and second-hand furniture. DIY sprays often miss eggs and deep harborages, so infestations rebound. Titan delivers a multi-visit, inspection-led program that targets eggs, nymphs and adults for complete, lasting relief. Our certified teams work discreetly across Hyderabad with clear safety guidance and a straightforward service guarantee.

Why Choose Titan

  • Lifecycle kill strategy: Precise chemistry + steaming/heat where suitable, with planned follow-ups.
  • Discreet service: Professional conduct and unmarked vehicles to protect your privacy.
  • Clear, written plan: Free inspection, itemized quote and what-to-expect timeline.
  • Safe for homes & businesses: Govt-approved products and detailed re-entry instructions.
  • Coverage everywhere: South/West/East/North/Central Hyderabad, plus Gurugram, Noida, Faridabad & Ghaziabad.

Our Treatment Method (What Actually Works)

  • Pre-treatment inspection and spread assessment (adjacent rooms if needed).
  • Two trained technicians for speed, coverage and quality control.
  • Targeted application to seams, cracks, bed frames, skirtings and voids.
  • Steam/heat on fabric seams and furniture where suitable.
  • HEPA vacuuming of visible bugs/eggs; safe bag disposal.
  • Dusting of sockets, cracks and crevices where appropriate.
  • Monitoring traps to track post-treatment activity.
  • Re-entry after dry time; avoid mopping treated skirtings for ~7 days.

Program Steps (2–3 Visits Typical)

  1. Inspection: Beds, sofas, headboards, wardrobes, baseboards, sockets and wall joints.
  2. Room mapping: Identify spread; include adjacent rooms when needed.
  3. First service: Targeted chemistry + steam/heat + vacuuming.
  4. Follow-up #1 (10–14 days): Hit newly hatched bugs before they reproduce.
  5. Follow-up #2 (if required): For heavy/multi-room infestations.
  6. Prevention tips: Sealing and housekeeping guidance to reduce re-infestation risk.

Pricing & Plans

Cost depends on rooms treated, severity, and methods (chemical/steam/heat). One-off “quick fixes” rarely work for bed bugs; a planned series is the proven approach. Start with a free inspection and get a written, itemized quote—no surprises.

How to Prepare (Boosts Success)

  • Bag clutter near beds/sofas; keep sealed between visits.
  • Wash & dry linens/clothes on high heat; store in sealed bags.
  • Pull beds 6–8 inches from walls; remove bed-skirts touching floor.
  • Do not move items from infested rooms into clean rooms pre-treatment.
  • After treatment, follow re-entry and cleaning guidance precisely.
