Why professional cockroach control matters

Cockroaches contaminate food, spread bacteria, and trigger allergies. Hyderabad’s density, warmth and moisture allow German and American roaches to multiply quickly. Our targeted program removes visible roaches, hits hidden colonies, and helps stop re-infestation.

Common signs of a roach infestation

  • Musty odor around kitchens or store rooms
  • Droppings like black pepper in cabinets or under sinks
  • Egg cases (oothecae) near appliances and hinges
  • Fast night activity when lights switch on
  • Smear marks near drains or damp areas

Our proven 7-step process

  1. Inspection & species ID
  2. Custom plan (methods + visits)
  3. Gel bait in crevices
  4. Targeted residual spray
  5. Dusting voids/conduits
  6. Exclusion & hygiene tips
  7. Follow-up & re-inspection
